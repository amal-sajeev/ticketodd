The Challenge

Why We Need a Better System

schedule

Slow Manual Triage

Grievances are manually read, classified, and routed to departments — causing delays of days or weeks before any action begins.

visibility_off

No Transparency

Citizens have no way to track the status of their complaints or know which officer is handling them.

shuffle

No Pattern Detection

Systemic issues — like a broken pipeline affecting an entire block — go unnoticed because complaints are handled in isolation.

gavel

Limited Accountability

No mechanism to detect if officers are rubber-stamping, cherry-picking easy cases, or missing SLA deadlines.

Systemic Issue Detection

The Constellation Engine

Automatically clusters nearby same-department grievances using geospatial + semantic similarity, then generates a root-cause analysis.

  • hubClusters grievances by geographic proximity and content similarity
  • psychologyGPT-5 generates root-cause analysis for each cluster
  • trending_upReveals systemic problems hidden across individual complaints
  • assignment_indOfficers are assigned to the systemic issue, not just individual cases
Predictive Intelligence

Forecasting Grievance Spikes
