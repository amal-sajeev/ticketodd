Process

End-to-End Flow

From filing to resolution in four stages — AI handles the heavy lifting at every step

1

Citizen Files

Citizens submit grievances via web form or AI chatbot in English, Odia, or Hindi. Supports photo/audio/video attachments up to 50 MB, auto GPS tagging, and anonymous filing.

2

AI Classifies

GPT-5-mini instantly classifies each grievance across four dimensions: department routing, priority level, citizen sentiment, and resolution tier — all in under 3 seconds.

3

Officer Resolves

Officers receive AI-drafted resolutions built from RAG context. Complex cases are decomposed into cross-department sub-tasks with independent SLA tracking.

4

Citizen Confirms

Citizens review the resolution and confirm or request further help. Self-resolvable cases (40%) are closed via AI guidance without officer involvement. Feedback is captured for quality tracking.

Smart Resolution

Three-Tier Resolution

🤖

Self-Resolvable

The AI chatbot provides immediate guidance using RAG-powered knowledge of past resolutions, government schemes, and department contacts. Citizens receive actionable steps and relevant scheme recommendations without waiting for an officer.

Avg. resolution: < 5 minutes  •  Satisfaction: 4.2/5
👤

Officer Required

Grievances are routed to the appropriate department officer with an AI-drafted resolution and full context. Officers review, edit, and submit the resolution. Complex cases are decomposed into trackable sub-tasks across departments.

Avg. resolution: 3–5 days  •  SLA compliance: 92%
⚠️

Escalation

High-complexity or cross-department issues are escalated to senior authorities with full audit trails. The system identifies systemic patterns — if 50+ similar grievances cluster in one area, it auto-flags for administrative review and priority allocation.

Avg. resolution: 7–14 days  •  Threshold: 50+ similar cases

How it works: The AI computes the score at filing time by analysing grievance text, citizen location, historical resolution data, and seasonal context. The five weighted dimensions are combined into a single number that determines queue position, SLA deadlines, and escalation thresholds — ensuring the most impactful cases are addressed first.
Why it matters: Traditional systems rely on manual priority assignment, which is inconsistent across officers and districts. The Impact Score eliminates bias, surfaces urgent cases that would otherwise be buried, and gives administrators a single metric to track district-wide responsiveness — all without any manual input from the filing citizen.

Spam Detection & Officer Accountability

Multi-signal fraud detection prevents abuse of the grievance system, while behavioral analytics flag officers with suspicious resolution patterns for admin review

shield Spam Detection Pipeline

Five independent signals are combined into a composite fraud score for every grievance submission

  • schedule Frequency Analysis — rapid submission pattern detection
  • content_copy Duplicate Detection — exact and near-match text comparison
  • compare Semantic Similarity — embedding-based cross-complaint matching
  • language IP & GPS Check — geolocation vs. claimed district verification
  • photo_camera Image Forensics — reverse image search for stock photo detection
monitoring Officer Anomaly Detection

Behavioral analytics continuously monitor officer actions and flag patterns that suggest misconduct or negligence

  • speed Rubber-Stamping — batch resolutions in impossibly short timeframes

  • content_paste Generic Resolutions — copy-paste detection in resolution text
  • do_not_disturb Inactivity Alerts — officers with no activity beyond threshold
  • gavel Audit Trail — complete action log for accountability review

Security & Tech Stack

Five layers of defense protect the system from abuse, unauthorized access, and data leakage — built on a modern, production-ready stack

security Security Headers

Content Security Policy, X-Frame-Options, X-XSS-Protection, and strict CORS configuration on every response

speed Rate Limiting

SlowAPI enforces per-IP and per-user rate limits to prevent brute-force attacks and API abuse

lock Authentication

JWT tokens with bcrypt password hashing. Role-based access control for citizen, officer, and admin roles
